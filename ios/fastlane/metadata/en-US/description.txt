Word Wheel Quest blends the satisfaction of crosswords with the tactile fun of a letter wheel. Each puzzle gives you a themed crossword grid and a circle of letters—connect them in the right order to spell every answer and complete the board.

HOW IT WORKS
• Read the clue — tap a numbered cell to see what word belongs there.
• Spin your strategy — letters sit on a wheel; swipe through them to build each answer.
• Fill the grid — correct words lock into the crossword pattern until the puzzle is complete.
• Need a nudge? Spend hint points to reveal help when you are stuck.
• Shuffle the wheel when you want a fresh look at the same letters.

THEMED PUZZLES
From calm morning rituals to richer word adventures, every level has a title and mood of its own. Grids stay readable, clues stay fair, and the pastel artwork keeps play relaxed—not stressful.

FEATURES
• Crossword-style grids with numbered clue cells
• Circular letter wheel for spelling each answer
• Hint system with point-based assists
• Shuffle, word list, and in-game tools
• No account required to start playing
• Designed for iPhone and iPad
