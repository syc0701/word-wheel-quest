Welcome to Word Wheel Quest—a cozy word adventure where crossword clues meet a letter wheel.

Need a calm break from a busy day? Read each clue, swipe letters on the wheel, and watch the crossword fill in. No noisy menus. No ticking clock (unless you turn the timer on). Just clear clues, smooth swipes, and that little spark when the right word clicks into place.

CLUES ARE THE HEART OF THE GAME
Every word has a real crossword-style clue. Swipe the clue strip to move between answers, tap a numbered cell for its hint, and use the clue—not guesswork alone—to solve the puzzle. That’s what sets Word Wheel Quest apart: thoughtful wordplay guided by meaning.

HOW TO PLAY
Swipe around the letter wheel to spell words. Match them to the grid, follow the clues, and fill every space at your own pace. It’s easy to learn, satisfying to master, and perfect for a few minutes—or a whole evening.

BONUS WORDS = EXTRA COINS
Spelled a real word that isn’t on the puzzle grid? That’s a Word Discovery! We reward you with a bonus coin right away for each new find. Collect those surprise words in your treasure chest and open them anytime to check their meaning in the dictionary.

WHEN YOU NEED A LITTLE HELP
Stuck? A gentle hint can light up a letter so you never feel stranded. Want a fresh look at the same letters? Give the wheel a playful shuffle and try a new path. Curious about a word you found? Peek at its meaning and keep learning while you play.

WHY PLAYERS LOVE IT
• Real crossword clues for every word — our strength
• Swipeable clue strip — stay focused on the next answer
• Relaxing, not stressful — play when you want, pause when you need
• Bonus rewards — earn coins for real words that aren’t on the puzzle
• Always something new — season levels to climb and daily puzzles waiting for you
