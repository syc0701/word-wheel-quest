Welcome to Word Wheel Quest—a cozy word adventure made purely for fun.

Need a calm break from a busy day? Open the wheel, connect a few letters, and watch the crossword come to life. No noisy menus. No ticking clock. Just a clear board, smooth swipes, and that little spark when the right word clicks into place.

HOW TO PLAY
Swipe around the letter wheel to spell words. Match them to the grid, follow the clues, and fill every space at your own pace. It’s easy to learn, satisfying to master, and perfect for a few minutes—or a whole evening.

WHEN YOU NEED A LITTLE HELP
Stuck? A gentle hint can light up a letter so you never feel stranded. Want a fresh look at the same letters? Give the wheel a playful shuffle and try a new path. Curious about a word you found? Peek at its meaning and keep learning while you play.

BONUS FINDS ALONG THE WAY
Every now and then you’ll invent a real word that isn’t on the board. That’s a treasure! Collect those surprise finds, earn a little coin reward for each new discovery, and revisit them anytime in your treasure chest.

WHY PLAYERS LOVE IT
• Relaxing, not stressful — play when you want, pause when you need
• Clean and inviting — the board stays simple so the words shine
• Always something new — season levels to climb and daily puzzles waiting for you
• Made for everyone — from quick coffee breaks to long, quiet puzzle nights
