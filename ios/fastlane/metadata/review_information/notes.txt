Word Wheel Quest is a crossword-style word puzzle. Tap a numbered cell to read its clue, then connect letters on the circular wheel to spell the answer into the grid. No login is required for basic play.

Play-screen tools (reviewers):
• Hint (lightbulb, left of the wheel): spends coins to reveal one letter on the grid.
• Treasure chest (under Hint): lists bonus words you found that are not on the puzzle; tap a word to open the dictionary definition sheet.
• Dictionary (book icon, right of the wheel): look up the selected word’s meaning.
• Shuffle (tornado icon, under Dictionary): rearranges letters on the wheel with a short animation.

Bonus words: spelling a real dictionary word that is not on the board awards +1 coin once per unique word and stores it in the treasure chest.

Test notes:
• Open any Season Journey puzzle (e.g. Level 15+).
• Tap numbered cells for clues; select letters on the wheel to form words.
• Try Hint, Treasure, Dictionary, and Shuffle as described above.
